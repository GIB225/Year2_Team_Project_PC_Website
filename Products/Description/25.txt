With a slim and light design, the HP 255 Laptop keeps up with mobile workstyles. The stunning display offers plenty of room for work or streaming video thanks to its slim border design and large screen-to-body ratio. This laptop has a USB Type-C data connector, an RJ-45 port, and an HDMI port so you can connect all of your accessories to it. It is also made to meet the needs of any company. This laptop features both built in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth 5.
